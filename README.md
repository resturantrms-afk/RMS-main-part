# 🍽️ SmartDine: Multi-Platform Restaurant Management System

A comprehensive, multi-platform solution designed to streamline restaurant operations, enhance customer experience, and leverage AI for data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Overview

SmartDine is an integrated ecosystem that connects customers, staff, and management in real-time. By utilizing **QR code integration** for seamless ordering and **AI-powered analytics** for reporting, the system optimizes the entire lifecycle of a restaurant order—from placement to payment.

## 👥 User Roles & Features

The system is structured into five distinct interfaces, each tailored to specific operational needs:

### 📱 1. Customer
* **QR Access:** Customers simply scan a QR code at their table to access the digital menu without downloading an app.
* **Self-Ordering:** Browse items, customize orders, and submit them directly to the kitchen.
* **Status Tracking:** Real-time updates on order preparation.

### 📋 2. Waiter
* **Manual Entry:** Provides flexibility for customers who prefer traditional service or do not have smartphones.
* **Service Dashboard:** A dedicated window showing "Ready to Serve" notifications.
* **Table Tracking:** Clear indicators of which table an order belongs to, ensuring fast and accurate delivery.

### 👨‍🍳 3. Kitchen
* **Order Management:** A digital display of incoming orders prioritized by time.
* **Prep Status:** Ability to mark items as "In Progress" or "Ready," instantly notifying the waiter.

### 💰 4. Cashier
* **Billing & Payments:** Centralized interface to process transactions, apply discounts, and issue receipts.
* **Order Reconciliation:** Final verification of orders before closing the table.

### 📊 5. Admin (Management)
* **System Configuration:** Manage menus, pricing, and staff accounts.
* **AI Reporting:** Advanced module that uses **Data Mining** and **Artificial Intelligence** to analyze sales trends, peak hours, and customer preferences.
* **Business Intelligence:** Automated reports to help optimize inventory and staffing.

---

## 🤖 AI & Data Mining

The heart of the reporting system lies in its ability to transform raw data into actionable insights:
* **Trend Analysis:** Identify which dishes are performing best and at what times.
* **Demand Forecasting:** Predict busy periods to assist in staff scheduling.
* **Customer Behavior:** Analyze ordering patterns to create personalized promotions or menu adjustments.

---

## 🛠️ System Architecture

* **Platform:** Multi-platform (Web / Mobile / Tablet)
* **Access Point:** QR Code URL Redirection
* **Backend:** Real-time database for instant sync between roles.
* **Analysis Engine:** AI/ML models for reporting and data mining.

---

## 🔄 Workflow

1.  **Initiation:** Customer scans QR code OR Waiter creates a manual order.
2.  **Preparation:** Kitchen receives the order and updates status to "Ready."
3.  **Delivery:** Waiter sees the "Ready" notification and delivers it to the specified table.
4.  **Settlement:** Cashier processes the final payment.
5.  **Optimization:** Admin reviews AI-generated reports to improve restaurant efficiency.
